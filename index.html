Research Interests

My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, and pattern analysis. A significant part of my work focuses on 3D and multimodal medical imaging, where I develop methods to extract meaningful patterns and structure from complex and high-dimensional data in clinical environments. I’m particularly motivated by challenges in data-scarce settings and aim to design approaches that are efficient and applicable in real-world scenarios.
